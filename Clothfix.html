Clothfix

A lot of times, when we're travelling, we face issues with small part of cloth tearing or becoming loose or overstretched. A sewing kit is generally carried around considering it will be used in such places; but this takes up too much time and sometimes people dont even know how to use them, a few times they don't know how to needle the thread too! Stee is a on-the-go cloth fixing tool that eliminates complexity and effort for making small amends to cloths or bags when in a hurry.
