Hi, I am Trishala. Game designer, Design researcher & a storyteller

Hi, I am a game designer and I make games that are relatable to a variety of audiences. I converge my multidisciplinary knowledge into interactive play experiences. My chief skill lies in threading different experiences together to deliver something unique.
Adaptive to most situations, I am a quick learner and take up responsibilities. Describe me as a generalist who can be fitted anywhere. I am known to get work done and make sure people around me are also on track.
My past work experiences have taught me to think on my feet and focus on the deliverables. As much as I love research, my strength lies in reading the outcome and analyzing data. In December 2020, I conducted an independent study relating to the urban Indian elderly and their contribution in craft sector. My degree project was with Nivea India, where I studied consumer behaviour to generate a very relevant range of packaging design. As a part of my system design subject in 7th semester of college, I studied waste management and designed a concept for e-waste management which I got to present at 'Maker Mela'. The most recent academic research that I conducted was relating to the panipuri (food) cart at NID.
